OVERVIEW

This app will be a software to help not only housewives but can be used from people who need to cook to be proactive in life to professional chefs. They include not only recipes but also detailed instructional videos from chefs and home-cook everywhere.

app features

Introduction

This App will be sold for free on the App store. They will be integrated AI guidance for beginners and voice assist technology. This App will be for mobile only and we also have our own website that works like an online app.

It is also designed to serve any person that has the needs of cooking no matter cooking skills or experience, anyone can cook. However, our major target users are people between 20 and 35, mostly students and busy adults who are lack of cooking skills and experience.
